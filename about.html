Our Story

About Us

Chess has deep roots in Ethiopia. We are here to grow them.

Ethiopian Chess is a community dedicated to building a thriving chess culture in Ethiopia — connecting players across the diaspora, and developing local talent.

Our Mission

We believe Ethiopia can produce world-class chess players. Our goal is to create the competitive infrastructure, community, and resources that give Ethiopian players — at home and abroad — a pathway to reach their potential.

We run regular online tournaments on Lichess open to all, with plans for over-the-board events as the community grows. Every tournament is documented, every result recorded, and every great game celebrated.

Our Tournaments

Freestyle Friday — A weekly Chess960 blitz event on Lichess. Open to all skill levels. Every Friday.

Over-the-board events are in development. If you are interested in helping organize or sponsor an OTB event, please get in touch.

Join Us

Whether you are a beginner who just learned the rules or an experienced player looking for competition, there is a place for you here. Follow us, join the next tournament, and be part of what we are building.
