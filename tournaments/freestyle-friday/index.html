Freestyle Friday

Every Friday on Lichess — a new position, a new champion.

Freestyle Friday is our weekly Chess960 blitz Swiss tournament on Lichess. Every Friday brings a fresh random starting position, leveling the playing field between experienced players and newcomers alike. 5+2 time control, open to all.

How to Join

1. Create a free account at lichess.org

2. Watch this page or our Telegram group for the tournament link each Friday

3. Join before the start time and play
